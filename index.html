Goo Pop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Goo Pop mobile application (“the App”, “we”, “our”) handles your information.

What we collect

Goo Pop does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information to its developer or any third party. The App functions entirely offline.

What is stored on your device

The App saves the following data locally on your device only, using Android’s standard SharedPreferences storage:

  • A player display name you optionally enter when achieving a high score (capped at 12 characters).
  • A local top-10 leaderboard of your own scores.
  • Your audio and gameplay preferences.

This information never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App deletes all of it.

Permissions

Goo Pop does not request location, contacts, microphone, camera, storage, or any other sensitive Android permission. The only system service it uses is the standard Android audio output for sound effects.

Third-party services

The current version of the App does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or third-party services.

A future version may integrate Google Play Games Services to enable optional global leaderboards. If that integration is added, sign-in will be optional, will use your existing Google Play Games account, and will be subject to Google’s own Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). Score submissions through Play Games Services will only include your score number and the account name you have configured in Play Games — not any other personal data.

Children’s privacy

Goo Pop does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain advertising, in-app purchases, or external links.

Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact
